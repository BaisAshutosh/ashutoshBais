Here is a little about my journey!
I started with a civil engineering degree, but while exploring different fields, I discovered my interest in the field of design. Always up for a challenge, my journey hasn't been a straight line, but every detour has become a valuable lesson. Now, I'm on the brink of completing my M.DES at the National Institute of Design. These past two years have been an incredible learning adventure. In my prior role as a civil engineer, I collaborated with diverse teams and frequently engaged with locals during field visits. This experience strengthened my ability to manage diverse teams, integrate multiple perspectives, and enhance my communication skills.

What excites me most? Understanding the user-centric approach and creating user experiences that make a difference! Juggling part-time work and freelance design has given me a unique perspective.
